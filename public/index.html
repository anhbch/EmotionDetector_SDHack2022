How to use Eemodoro

    Allow access and turn on your camera.
    Click 'Start' to begin a 30-minute timer.
    Work on your task until the time is up.
    Keep your camera open all the time.
    If you want to reset the timer, click 'Reset'.
    After 30 minutes, Eemodoro will detect your dominated emotion during your work.
    Click 'Show Result' to see how you were feeling during your task.
    Take a break, enjoy our solution, and reward yourself for working so hard!
